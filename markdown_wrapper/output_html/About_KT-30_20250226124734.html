Transportation

The Millennity is conveniently situated at the transportation hub of Kowloon East, easily accessible by public transport including the MTR, bus, and taxi. It takes approximately 8 minutes to walk to the Millennity from either Ngau Tau Kok or Kwun Tong MTR stations.

 
By MTR:
From Ngau Tau Kok MTR Station
From Kwun Tong MTR Station

 

By Bus:

There are bus stops along Kwun Tong Road. You can use the links below to find buses that stop at Meyer Industrial Building, which is located directly in front of the Millennity.

Kowloon Motor Bus Co. Ltd.
Citybus Ltd.

 
By Taxi:

A taxi from the Clear Water Bay (CWB) campus to the Millennity costs about HK$120 each way. Taxis can drop you off directly at the Millennity or at the Entrance Piazza on the CWB campus.
