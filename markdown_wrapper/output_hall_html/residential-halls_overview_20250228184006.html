We have a wide range of choices

We pride ourselves on having a wide variety of room types for students to choose from with 2,976 places in double or triple rooms for UG students in UG Halls I to VI and 1,568 places in single or double rooms for UG students in Chan Sui Kau and Chan Lam Moon Chun Hall (UG Hall VII), UG Hall VIII and UG Hall IX and TKO Jockey Club Hall.​

*Barrier-free means that our hall places are accessible for all residents, no matter with disabilities or not. Some of the features are as follows:

  • Wheelchair accessible
  • Unobstructed area adjacent to the door handle
  • Shower cubicle adjacent to toilet
  • Shower seat
  • Grab rails
  • Rotating mirror
  • Emergency bell near the toilet seat

If you require any special assistance from us, please contact us at ughousing@ust.hk (for undergraduates) or pghousing@ust.hk (for research postgraduates).
