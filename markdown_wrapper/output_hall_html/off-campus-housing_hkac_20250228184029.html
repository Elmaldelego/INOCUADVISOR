Hong Kong Adventist College (HKAC) is located at 1111, Clear Water Bay Road, Sai Kung.​​

The student hostels in Hong Kong Adventist College is close to the HKUST campus and provide accommodation for HKUST students. A room, fully furnished with air-conditioner, is shared by 4 students. There is a bathroom with toilet on each floor and a pantry located on G/F of each wing of the dormitory. A dinning center in Hong Kong Adventist College which provides vegetarian food.​
